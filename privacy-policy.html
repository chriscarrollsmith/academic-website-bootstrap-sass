Privacy Policy

Cookies

Your IP address, referral source, landing page, and similar details may be collected by Google Analytics for the purpose of analyzing traffic to this website.

Embedded content from other websites

Articles on this site may include embedded content (e.g. videos, images, articles, etc.). Embedded content from other websites behaves in the exact same way as if the visitor has visited the other website.

These websites may collect data about you, use cookies, embed additional third-party tracking, and monitor your interaction with that embedded content, including tracking your interaction with the embedded content if you have an account and are logged in to that website.

Who your data is shared with

Personal information the site collects will never be shared with any third party without your permission except in the event that you opt-in to receive email updates via an automated email service such as MailChimp.

What rights you have over your data

You may request to receive an exported file of the personal data the site’s database holds about you, including any data you have provided. You can also request that I erase any personal data the site holds about you. This does not include any data I am obliged to keep for administrative, legal, or security purposes.

How your data is protected

This site uses a secure SSL layer and secure third-party server owned by HostGator.

What data breach procedures I have in place
